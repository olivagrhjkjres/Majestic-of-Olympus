Majestic of Olympus

Challenge the gods of Olympus

Ascend to the divine realm in Majestic of Olympus! Challenge the gods in two legendary modes: Gem Rush: Immerse yourself in an endless match-3 odyssey! Strategically swap celestial gems to create explosive combos. Unleash 4 mythic powers—thunderous blasts, radiant beams, and more—to shatter the board. Earn ambrosia points to unlock mighty bonus bombs from Hephaestus' forge! Lucky Wheel: Feel Zeus’ favor! Spin the golden wheel of fortune for godly rewards—bonus points, rare bombs, and divine boosts. Will Athena grant wisdom or Ares bring explosive fortune? Forge your legend amidst clouds and constellations. Can you master Olympus’ infinite trials?
